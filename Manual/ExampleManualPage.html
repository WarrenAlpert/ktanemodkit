On the Subject of Remote Detonators

This bomb isn't JUST a jumble of buttons and knobs to fiddle with, there's more at play! Quick, take to the skies to thwart the bomber's remote detonator. Good thing the address of the local drone operation console is conveniently included in this module! :)

The bomber is on the move in the field, about to trigger a remote detonation. Command the nearby drone fleet to triangulate the remote detonator location, then jam it!


1. You, the expert, must connect to the web address of the drone operation console, displayed on the module.

A live aerial map is included on the module as well.

2. Identify which 3 drones have the necessary capabilities to jam the bomber's device.

3. Carefully maneuver the drones into a triangular formation around the bomber. The bomber may run away from drones when they hear them, or towards them to shoot them down.

Crashing into other drones will destroy them.
The bomber will shoot drones directly above them out of the sky.


Bomber's Remote Detonation Device:
If the serial number ends in an odd number, the remote detonator operates on a 2G radio.
Otherwise, if the there is a vowel in the bomb's serial number, the remote detonator operates on a 3G radio.
Otherwise, the remote detonator operates on a 4G radio.
